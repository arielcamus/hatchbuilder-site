A new way to start companies

The best startup ideas aren't invented in a brainstorm. They're discovered in the gap between what people need and what exists. Hatch systematically finds those gaps.

We believe the next generation of great companies will be built by small teams moving fast on validated signals — not by large teams guessing at what the market wants.

Signal over noise

Every idea is backed by demand data, not speculation. If people aren't already searching for it, complaining about it, or paying for a bad version of it — it's not ready.

Speed over polish

Ship the ugly version first. Validate with real users. Iterate with data. The market doesn't care about your architecture — it cares about your solution.

Leverage over headcount

AI changes what one person can build. Hatch is designed for founders who want to do more with less — not raise a Series A to figure out if the idea works.
